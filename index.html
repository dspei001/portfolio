Clear Funds

Clear Funds was a Hackathon project for Broward College in 2017. The idea was to create a site for an entity, Clear Funds, that would act as a sort of donation funds transparency system for participating non-profits or other charitable programs. The user is the donor who would be able to search for a participating organization and be presented with a list of services provided by that organization where the donor can then select and contribute to specific services of his or her choosing. This was intended to provide more transparency regarding the use of donation funds, discourage charity fraud, and provide a mechanism for people to find and make connections with vetted, trustworthy organizations, as well as report any suspected fraud.
