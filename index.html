About Me

I currently live in downtown Fort Lauderdale, Florida with my partner and my incredibly sweet Mini Goldendoodle, Jaxon. In my spare time, I try to learn new (to me) technologies. Currently, I'm learning GUI development using Java Swing, JavaScript, and navigating GitHub. For fun, I've been enjoying learning about scripting with AppleScript and automation.

Approach to development and design

I try to capture the essence of each project using a minimalist design model while also maintaining an attractive, easy-to-navigate experience for both the users and the people who will be reading my code. I take great care in maintaining organized, readable, and reusable code to the absolute best of my capability. I am eager to learn more about UI design and creating GUIs, as most of my coding projects are limited to console application. I am also currently expanding my knowledge on object-oriented design and UX concepts. I have a passion for usability as poor usability can make a product that was great in theory terrible in practice.

Histogram Generator

Histogram Generator generates a histogram in the user's chosen color scheme with given data entered by the user with uniform or normal distribution. The colors of the histogram change every ten bins for easy reading in the console. The bins are then listed with the number of values that fell into each bin. Lastly, a text file with the sample values is generated for reference.

Clear Funds

Clear Funds Page was a Hackathon project for Broward College in 2017. The idea was to create a site for an entity, Clear Funds, that would act as a sort of donation funds transparency system for participating non-profits or other charitable programs. The user is the donor who would be able to search for a participating organization and be presented with a list of services provided by that organization where the donor can then select and contribute to specific services of his or her choosing. This was intended to provide more transparency regarding the use of donation funds, discourage charity fraud, and provide a mechanism for people to find and make connections with vetted, trustworthy organizations, as well as report any suspected fraud.
